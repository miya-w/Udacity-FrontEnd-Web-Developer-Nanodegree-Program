How to work from home?

We started working from since March 2020.
It wasn't in our plan, Just one day, my partner told me his company told him "Tomorrow, everyone works from home." The next working day, we just start working on our dining table in our small apartment. The first week is the most unproductive days ever. We don't know how long it gonna be? Should we really buy the desk table and suck in our tiny living room? How to draw a line between working and break when we stay at home? When we finally got time to go to IKEA for setting up our home office on the first weekend, and we found lots of people had flocked to IKEA. Most of the desks, chairs and office furniture had been out of stock. We were so speechless and feel everything is so unreal. Fortuninary, our ordered table came to us by mail after a couple of days. in these days, we made lots of adjustments and comprising, so I sum up these working from home tips below through my personal experience.

Create a routine, and stick to the working hours.
Our body has a complex mechanism. Try stick to the schedule, make the routine will help your mind to transit to the working mode.

Make your own ritual
Since the boundary of office and home has been broken, the work and break are getting blur. Before we might have the commute as the connection between working and home. Now we might need to create a transition to help us to get into your working mind!

  • Try to dress up in the morning(Like the clothes you usually do to )
  • Early morning exercise
  • Morning mirror pep talk

Investment the tech and create the workspace
in remote work, all the task and communication rely on our tech equipment, so the stable and reliable tech is essential at this moment! Try to get a stable NBN, and examining your computer. You never want to disconnect whine you are in an important meeting. So if you want to keep your work goes smoothly and well communication, it's totally worth to make some investment.
